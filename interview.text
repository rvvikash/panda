Analyzing Sales DataYou are given a dataset containing sales data for a retail store. The dataset includes columns for Date,
Product ID, Quantity Sold, and Revenue. Your task is to perform the following analysis:
a) Calculate the total revenue generated by each product.
b) Identify the top 5 best-selling products based on total quantity sold.
c) Determine the average revenue per day for each month.
d) Visualize the monthly trend of total revenue over the year.Hint: 
You may need to use pandas for data manipulation and matplotlib or seaborn for data visualization.
